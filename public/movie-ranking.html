The Best Movies According to Kailey

My Top 3 Favorite Movies

Spirited Away

This movie will always have a special place in my heart. It and many other Ghibli movies will always be in my top favorites.

Hunt for the Wilderpeople

This movie always cracks me up. It never ceases to amuse me and make me cry. It's so wholesome

The Phantom of the Opera

I first saw this movie when I was 10. It has been one of my favorites ever since. The music is stunning and the vocals from Emmy Rossum made me want to learn to sing.
